Rushi's Studio

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026

Rushi's Studio is an iPhone app published by Rushi Vyas / Know Whats On.

What the app does

The app provides access to the live Studio project library, shared project and presentation pages when available, QR codes for Rushi's website and LinkedIn, and the live homepage experience inside the app.

Data the app handles

  • Browsing the app can request website and studio content from Know Whats On-operated services.
  • If you tap Copy, the selected link is written to your device clipboard using the system clipboard API.
  • If you tap Open, the selected link is opened using the system browser.
  • If you tap Share, iOS opens the system share sheet with the selected link.
  • If you choose to contact Rushi by email from the app or website content, your message is handled by your mail provider.

What the app does not do

  • The app does not sell personal data.
  • The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • The app does not create a mandatory in-app account for the public experience.
  • The app does not use in-app analytics or tracking SDKs in the current build.

Operational data

Like most web-backed apps, requests to the service may generate standard server logs such as IP address, timestamp, requested path, device or browser metadata, and security diagnostics. This information is used for service operation, reliability, and abuse prevention.
